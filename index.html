About Me

Hi! I’m Epe from Binangonan, Rizal, Philippines. I’m a fresh graduate from Jose Rizal University with experience as a freelance Front-End Developer. For my thesis, I independently built a full-stack MEAN application, handling both the front-end and back-end development on my own.

As a developer, I specialize in translating designs into functional, responsive, and user-friendly websites. I ensure that every project I work on is optimized for performance, creatively executed, and aligned with the client’s goals. I’m passionate about learning new technologies, staying updated with the latest trends, and continuously improving my skills.

I believe that collaboration is key to building great products. I work closely with stakeholders to understand their needs and translate them into effective system features — making sure projects are delivered efficiently and with quality.

Outside of coding, I value work-life balance. As developers, we often spend hours solving problems and debugging, so I make it a point to refresh my mind by playing guitar and enjoying video games. These hobbies not only keep me relaxed but also fuel my creativity and problem-solving skills.

I’m excited to grow in the tech industry, contribute to impactful projects, and continue pushing my boundaries as a developer.
